Who We Are

Te Mana o te Tangata is a grassroots movement born from the struggles of everyday people. We are fighting for a system based on collective wellbeing, economic justice, indigenous sovereignty, and environmental restoration — because the future belongs to us all, not just the privileged few.

Our Vision

  • 🏡 Housing for All — Affordable, community-led housing built on tikanga and sustainability.
  • 🌏 Economy for Wellbeing — Tax the rich, feed the people, invest in collective prosperity.
  • 🪶 Indigenous Sovereignty — Honour Te Tiriti. Return land, power, and decision-making to iwi and hapū.
  • 🌿 Environmental Justice — Protect and restore the whenua with regenerative and indigenous-led solutions.

Join the Movement

True change begins with people like you. Whether you can kōrero with your community, organize events, create art, or simply stand with us — every voice matters.
