Volunteer With Us

Te Mana o te Tangata is powered by people like you. This movement is about reclaiming dignity, building community, and fighting for systems that serve us all — not just the wealthy few.

There are many ways you can contribute:

  • 🎨 Create artwork, graphics, or videos
  • 📢 Help spread awareness on social media
  • 🧠 Research and write policy explainers
  • 📋 Assist with events, stalls, or actions
  • 🧑‍💻 Support with web and admin tasks
  • 💬 Host local kōrero or lead community actions

Bring your energy, your skills, your care. We’ll build this together.
